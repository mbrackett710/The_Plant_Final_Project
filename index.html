The Plant is proud to serve tasty, wholesome food that’s sustainable and economical. Everything we serve is totally organic and local sourced, which means the freshest produce from around the bay area, free range poultry, and seafood from sources that are both wild and sustainable.

At the Plant, we serve California Cuisine with an Asian flair. Juices and smoothies, salads, sandwiches, pastas, poultry, seafood and deserts– all organic, ethically produced and freshly made. We’re so committed to nurturing the relationship between humans and the earth that even our restaurants themselves are constructed in the greenest way possible, lowering carbon emissions.

Why Organic ?

Organic produce employs pesticides and fertilizers processed from natural sources, while conventional farming utilizes strong chemically synthesized agents, many of which are shown to be damaging to health and the environment. We’re proud to support the organic foods movement, and to serve food that is healthy for our customers and the local ecosystem.

Organic, locally farmed produce is usually picked when it’s ripe and at its peak flavor and nutritional value, instead of when picked early and shipped over long distances. Organic certification ensures that food has been grown and handled carefully to avoid the use of unwanted chemicals, preservatives, antibiotics, growth hormones and GMO’s. Additionally, organic farming uses less water and has much less negative impact on the environment than conventional farming. By supporting local farmers, The Plant helps ensure local biodiversity and the economic survival of small, family farmers.

Catering

The Plant offers a wide array of catering options with a diverse set of flavorful, fresh and healthy choices sourced from your local farms. Our catering menu ranges from fresh seasonal juices and salads, sandwiches, appetizers and entrees to delicious house made organic desserts and pastries.

Our pastries, cupcakes, tarts and cakes are exquisite - made from the highest quality ingredients. Susanne, our pastry chef, is truly talented and loves what she does and the ingredients she works with. She creates special desserts for the occasion working from a variety of recipes to include classic colorful presentations as well as delicious raw and vegan options. Contact us to find out what special seasonal items are available the time of your event.

We cater to small and large events, breakfast, lunch and dinner. Whether it's formal, informal, a small business lunch, buffet for 100 or other kind of event, we work to provide the best to suit the occasion. For larger events or special menu requests, we typically ask for the final order 48+ hours prior to event though will work to accommodate as best we can given your timeframe.
